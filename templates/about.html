About Recommendo

What is Recommendo?

Recommendo is a content-based book recommendation system built with simplicity and privacy in mind. It uses natural language processing techniques like TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to suggest books based on your favorite titles—no accounts, no databases, just straightforward recommendations.

Why Recommendo?

Many recommendation systems overcomplicate things with heavy databases, user profiles, and tracking. Recommendo respects your privacy and keeps the experience focused on what matters: finding your next great read quickly and easily.

How Does It Work?

When you enter a book title, Recommendo compares its metadata — genres, descriptions, authors — against a curated dataset. It then computes similarity scores and suggests the five most similar books, helping you discover titles you'll love.

Who Built This?

This project was completed as part of the semester project for the Computer Science(Year II / Semester II) program at Kathmandu University. It was developed by a dedicated team of five members Jebisha, Prajwal, Saksham, Salina, and Shiva, under the supervision of Mr. Suman Shrestha.
